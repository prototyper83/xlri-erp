The discussion tree

A transcript is a wall of text nobody rereads.

The tree shows which branch is starved of evidence, which one everyone quietly abandoned, and where two threads are the same question. Click a node.

Ink is a first-class citizen: write or draw on the board and handwriting binds to the node beneath it.
